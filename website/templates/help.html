Connect 4 Tutorial

Step 1: Start the Game

Start the Connect 4 game by clicking on the 'Play' button.

Step 2: Drop Tokens

Take turns with your opponent to drop your colored tokens into the columns. The goal is to connect four of your tokens vertically, horizontally, or diagonally.

Step 3: Win the Game

If you successfully connect four of your tokens, you win the game! If the board fills up before either player achieves four in a row, the game is a draw.

Rules:

  1. The game is played on a adjustable grid initially consists of 12 columns wide and 7 rows high.
  2. Players take turns dropping one colored disc (blue or orange in random) from the top into the grid.
  3. The pieces fall straight down, occupying the lowest available space within the column.
  4. The game continues until one player forms a line of four of their own colored discs horizontally, vertically, or diagonally.
  5. If the grid is filled with discs and no player has won, the game is a draw.

For more detailed rules and strategies, you can refer to the official Connect 4 rulebook or online resources.
